Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our Finance internship program

01

Why is finance knowledge important?

▲

Finance knowledge is crucial as it forms the backbone of business operations and personal financial well-being. Understanding finance enables individuals and organizations to make informed decisions about resource allocation, investment opportunities, risk management, and long-term planning. In today's complex economic environment, financial literacy helps navigate market fluctuations, evaluate investment options, and build sustainable wealth. For businesses, strong financial management ensures operational efficiency, profitability, and growth potential. Whether you're a student, professional, or entrepreneur, financial expertise opens doors to better career opportunities, business success, and personal financial security.

02

What are the prospects of finance as a career?

▼

Finance offers excellent career prospects with diverse roles such as Financial Analyst, Investment Banker, Portfolio Manager, Risk Analyst, Financial Planner, and Corporate Finance Manager. The average salary for finance professionals in India ranges from ₹5-25 lakhs per annum depending on experience, specialization, and organization. Finance professionals are in high demand across industries, offering job security and significant advancement opportunities. With the growing complexity of global financial markets and increasing focus on financial management, skilled finance professionals continue to be highly sought after.

03

Why Persevex?

▼

At Persevex we place an emphasis on equipping our interns with the practical skills necessary to succeed. This doesn't mean however, that the subject's theory is exempted from our course. We will teach you the necessary theoretical concepts and practical skills to succeed. We will also let you get hands-on experience from our top associated mentors who are distinguished in their respective fields. Our finance program is designed by industry experts with years of experience in investment banking, corporate finance, and financial analysis.

04

Do I need prior experience in finance?

▼

No prior experience is required. Our program is designed for beginners as well as those with some experience looking to enhance their skills. We start with the fundamentals of finance and progressively move to advanced concepts, ensuring everyone can follow along regardless of their background. However, basic knowledge of mathematics and economics would be beneficial.

05

Will I receive a certificate after completing the internship?

▼

Yes, upon successful completion of the internship program, you will receive three certificates: a course completion certificate, an internship certificate, and an outstanding performance certificate (based on your performance during the program). These certificates are recognized in the industry and will add value to your resume.

Week 1: Financial Fundamentals

  • Introduction to Financial Markets
  • Financial Statements Analysis
  • Time Value of Money
  • Risk and Return Concepts
  • Financial Ratios and Interpretation

Week 2: Financial Modeling

  • Excel for Financial Modeling
  • Building Income Statement Models
  • Balance Sheet and Cash Flow Modeling
  • Forecasting Techniques
  • Sensitivity Analysis and Scenario Planning

Week 3: Valuation and Investment Analysis

  • Discounted Cash Flow (DCF) Analysis

  • Mergers and Acquisitions Modeling
  • Investment Decision Making

  • Corporate Finance Strategies
  • Risk Management Techniques
  • Financial Planning and Analysis
  • FinTech and Financial Innovation
